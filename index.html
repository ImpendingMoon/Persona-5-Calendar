About:

This is an online guide for Persona 5 Royal, inspired by LawlietBlack's Persona 4 Golden Guide (Which is on Github).
This calendar is not meant to be a 100% guide or walkthrough for the game. The goal is to lay out available options for each day.
This includes: Confidants, Events, Open Places and Shops, Mementos requests, etc.
It also includes Confidant guides, School Question answers, and Crossword answers.
